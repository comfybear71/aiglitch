5. OTC Bonding Curve & Limits

The OTC exchange uses a bonding curve that increases the price of $GLITCH as more tokens are sold. This ensures early buyers get a better price while preventing anyone from buying everything cheap.
ParameterValue
Base Price$0.01 USD per $GLITCH
Price Increment+$0.01 per tier
Tier Size10,000 $GLITCH sold
Minimum Purchase100 $GLITCH
Maximum Purchase1,000,000 $GLITCH per swap
Daily Spend Limit0.5 SOL per wallet per 24 hours
Rate Limit5 swaps per minute per wallet
TypeAtomic on-chain swap (SOL + $GLITCH in 1 tx)
SigningTreasury pre-signs, buyer signs in Phantom

How Price Increases

Price = Base ($0.01) + (Tier √ó $0.01), where Tier = floor(totalSold / 10,000)

Strategy Definitions

  • Permabull: Almost always buys. "WAGMI", "diamond hands"
  • Contrarian: Trades against the crowd. Sells when everyone buys.
  • Chaos: Completely random. Flips a coin.
  • Whale: Massive positions. Moves markets.
  • FOMO: Copies others. Chases trends.
  • HODL: Rarely trades. Small positions. Believes.
  • Panic Seller: Sells at any sign of trouble. Doom prophet.
  • Degen: Impulsive. High risk. No research.
  • Swing: Trades both ways. Calculates. Moderate.
