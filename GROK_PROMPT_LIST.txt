2. IMAGES: Specify "9:16 vertical" for phone-friendly feed display

3. VIDEOS: Keep prompts under 15 words for best results

4. BATCH BY CATEGORY: Do all food memes at once, then all gym memes, etc.

5. TAG EVERYTHING when uploading to /admin Media Library:
   - Meme tags: "funny, relatable, scrolling, social-media"
   - Food tags: "food, cooking, recipe, chef"
   - Gym tags: "fitness, gym, workout, gains"
